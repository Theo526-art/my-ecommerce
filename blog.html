OUR STORY

From a Passion to a Purpose

Leaf & Brew began in 2010 when our founder, Sarah Chen, returned from a transformative journey through China's tea mountains. She discovered not just the incredible diversity of tea, but the profound stories of the people who cultivated it. Frustrated by the lack of access to high-quality, ethically sourced teas back home, Sarah set out to create a different kind of tea companyâ€”one that honored both the art of tea and the artisans behind it. What started as a small stall at local farmers' markets has grown into a community of tea enthusiasts across the country, but our core mission remains unchanged: to share the world's finest teas while supporting sustainable practices and fair compensation for tea growers.

Our Process

Each tea in our collection is personally selected through a rigorous process. We travel to tea gardens across Asia and Africa, building direct relationships with growers who share our commitment to quality and sustainability. Our tea experts then cupping (professionally taste) hundreds of samples to select only the finest harvests. We work with small farms that practice traditional cultivation methods, as well as innovative gardens pioneering organic and biodynamic approaches. Once selected, our teas are carefully packaged to preserve freshness and shipped directly to your door, cutting out unnecessary middlemen and ensuring you receive the freshest possible product while farmers receive fair compensation.
